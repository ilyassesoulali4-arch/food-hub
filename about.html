About FoodHub

Connecting people with delicious food since 2020

Our mission is to make quality food accessible, affordable, and delivered fast to everyone.

Our Story

How FoodHub began and where we're heading

Founded in 2020

FoodHub started with a simple idea: make it easier for people to enjoy their favorite food without leaving home. What started as a small project connecting just 50 restaurants has grown into a platform serving thousands of customers daily.

💡

Our Vision

To be the most loved food delivery platform that empowers restaurants and delights customers with quality, speed, and reliability.

🎯

Our Mission

Deliver happiness through food, one order at a time. We connect hungry people with quality restaurants and fast, reliable delivery.

💚

Our Values

Quality, Speed, Reliability, Customer-first, Sustainability, and Community building drive everything we do.

Our Milestones

Key moments in the FoodHub journey

2020

🚀 FoodHub Launches

We started with a vision to revolutionize food delivery in the city. Our first month saw partnerships with 50 restaurants and 1,000 happy customers.

2021

📈 10,000 Customers Milestone

Our network expanded to 200+ partner restaurants. We introduced loyalty rewards and launched our mobile app on iOS and Android.

2022

🌟 Expansion Beyond City

We expanded to 5 new cities and reached 50,000+ active customers. Introduced premium membership with exclusive discounts.

2023

🏆 Industry Recognition

Won "Best Food Delivery App" award. Served our 1 millionth order. Implemented AI-powered recommendation system.

2024

🚀 Today & Tomorrow

500+ partner restaurants, 50,000+ regular customers. Continuing to innovate with new features and expanding to more cities!

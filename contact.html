Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find what you're looking for?

❓ How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered within 30 minutes. Delivery time depends on your location and current order volume.

❓ What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and cash on delivery in selected areas.

❓ Can I cancel my order?

Yes! You can cancel orders within 5 minutes of placing. Contact our support team immediately for cancellation requests after that.

❓ How can I become a restaurant partner?

We're always looking for great restaurants to partner with! Email us at partners@foodhub.com with your restaurant details and menu.

❓ Do you have vegetarian/vegan options?

Yes! Most of our partner restaurants offer vegetarian and vegan options. You can filter by dietary preferences in the menu.
